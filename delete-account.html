Delete Your Account

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your account and personal data for the app "Sidera Vedic Astrology".

---

1. How to Request Deletion

You can request account deletion by contacting us via email:

Email: support@sidera-astrology.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your account email address
  • Optional: additional details to help identify your account
---

2. What Happens After Your Request

Once we receive your request:

  • Your account will be deleted
  • Your personal data will be removed from our systems
---

3. Data That May Be Retained

We may retain certain information if required for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Compliance with applicable laws
---

4. Processing Time

We will process your request within 30 days, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
